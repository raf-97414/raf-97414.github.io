📸 Snap Drops AI Video Lenses – Now Your Selfies Come with Furry Friends & Flowers! 🌸🦝

Snapchat just cranked up the AI magic! 🪄 The app now features AI-powered Video Lenses—and yes, your Snaps will now come with cuddly raccoons and zoom-out flower shots. 🌸📸

🤖 What’s New?

🎥 Snap’s First AI Video Lenses – Powered by their own in-house generative video model (fancy, huh?).

  • 🦝 Raccoon: Snuggle with a digital raccoon.
