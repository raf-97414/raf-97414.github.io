🍎 Siri’s Fit Cousin: Health+

Apple is reportedly cooking up Health+, an AI-powered health coach designed to whip us into shape using data from our devices. Think of it as Siri’s athletic cousin who actually cares about your step count and kale intake. 🥗🏃‍♀️

📈 Personalized Pep Talks

Health+ will analyze your health data to provide personalized advice, nudging you towards healthier choices. Ate a whole pizza? 🍕 Expect a gentle (or not-so-gentle) reminder to maybe opt for a salad next time. 🥗

🍽️ Food Tracking: Because We Forget

Struggle to remember what you ate for breakfast? Health+ has your back with integrated food tracking, making it easier to log meals and keep tabs on your nutrition. Just be honest—yes, that midnight ice cream counts. 🍦

🎥 Doctor’s Orders: Now Streaming

To boost your health IQ, Apple plans to feature videos from real doctors within the app. It’s like having a medical drama in your pocket, minus the drama and with more credible advice. 🩺🎬

📅 Coming Soon-ish

While we’re all eager to meet our new AI fitness friend, Health+ is expected to debut with iOS 19.4 in spring or summer 2026. So, you’ve got some time to enjoy those donuts before the AI starts its gentle nagging. 🍩⏳

🤖 Conclusion: Embrace the AI Gains

With Health+, Apple’s blending technology and personal health in a way that could make us all a bit fitter, or at least more aware of our questionable habits. Get ready to welcome your new AI coach—because who doesn’t want a pocket-sized motivator reminding you to take the stairs? 🏋️‍♂️📱
