🧊 Roblox Drops ‘Cube’: AI That Makes 3D Stuff With Just a Prompt 🎨🤖

Rafelia

272

2025-03-18 05:30 +0530

🚨 Roblox just went full sci-fi! 🚨

They’ve launched “Cube”, an AI tool that lets creators make 3D objects with just a prompt! 🧊✨
➡️ “Generate an orange racing car with black stripes” 🏎️🟧
➡️ BOOM 💥 now it’s in your game 🎮.

And the best part?
Cube is open source! 💾 So even non-Roblox folks can tinker, build plug-ins, or make Cube smarter with their own datasets 🧑‍💻⚙️.

What else is Roblox cooking? 🍳

🔥 Mesh generation (in beta): Type a prompt, get a 3D model. Easy peasy 🟩
🗣️ Text-to-Speech: NPCs now have voices, no more awkward silence 🎙️
🎤 Speech-to-Text: Yell “MOVE FORWARD!” and your character listens 👂
💬 Text Generation: Make NPCs chatty or sassier than your squad 😏

But wait… there’s more! 🤯

🪵 Scene Generation (coming soon):

  • “Make a cozy forest in fall 🍂🌲”
  • AI builds the whole thing 🌄 (and yes, you can change the leaf colors too).

The Big Picture 🖼️

Roblox calls this the road to “4D creation” 🌀 — where objects, people, and worlds interact like it’s some Matrix-level stuff 😮‍💨.

“The fourth dimension is interaction,” says Roblox’s VP of Engineering, Nick Tornow 🤓.

🧐 But not everyone’s hyped…

⚠️ 30% of devs say AI could mess up the gaming space 😬,
and studies show 13.4% of gaming jobs might get hit by AI by 2026 🤖💼.

Meanwhile, Tencent’s out here dropping its own open source 3D generator like it’s an AI arms race 🏃‍♂️💨.

🎮 AI in gaming = fast builds, spicy debates.

Stay tuned, creators — the future is looking wild 🚀✨
